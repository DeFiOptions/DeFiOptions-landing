Why options?

Limit losses

Options present an alternative over stop orders, while keeping independence of underlying asset.

Leverage

Because options are much cheaper to purchase in comparison to the underlying asset, you can purchase more and get more upside potential.

Strategies

Options allow us the flexibility to come up with many strategies that both limit risk and maximize return.

Yield

Providing liquidity in stablecoin based pool allows diversification of capital allocation, exposing users to significantly lower risk than writing an actual option.
