Why options?

Limit losses

Options present an alternative over stop orders, while keeping independence of the underlying asset.

Leverage

Because options are much cheaper to purchase in comparison to the underlying asset, you can purchase more and get more upside potential.

Strategies

Options allow us the flexibility to come up with many strategies that both limit risk and maximize return.

Yield

Providing liquidity in stablecoin-based pool allows diversification of capital allocation, and exposing users to significantly lower risk than writing actual options.

Why DeFi Options?

Efficient Option Prices

Our flexible pricing model will allow traders to get DeFi options at the most efficient prices on the market.

Stablecoin Pool

Provide liquidity in stablecoins to mitigate impermanent loss and start earning stablecoin-based yield in any market condition.

Scalability & Flexibility

DeFi Options supports buying put and call options for a wide range of underlying assets selected and approved by the DAO.

Stablecoin-Settled Calls & Puts

Buy, sell and earn profits in stablecoins. Stablecoins allow simplicity while removing volatility risk for users.
